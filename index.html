Our Environmental Impact

Every bottle you purchase helps reduce plastic waste and supports our conservation initiatives.

How Your Purchase Makes a Difference

Plastic Reduction

Each reusable bottle prevents approximately 167 single-use plastic bottles from entering landfills and oceans annually.

Reforestation Efforts

We plant one tree for every 10 bottles sold, with over 25,000 trees planted since our founding in 2020.

Clean Water Initiatives

5% of our profits support clean water projects in communities around the world, providing safe drinking water to those in need.
