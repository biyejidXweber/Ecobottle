Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most commonly asked questions.

Standard orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary by location but generally range from 3-7 business days for domestic orders. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination country.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all our products. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return unused items in their original packaging for a full refund or exchange. Please note that customized products cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect.

Most of our bottles are dishwasher safe on the top rack, but we recommend hand washing for optimal longevity, especially for bottles with bamboo components or special finishes. Always check the care instructions that come with your specific bottle model.

For regular cleaning, use warm water and mild dish soap with a bottle brush. For deeper cleaning, fill the bottle with a solution of one part white vinegar to five parts water, let it sit overnight, then rinse thoroughly. Bottle cleaning tablets are also effective for removing stubborn odors or stains.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account on our website to view your order status and tracking details. If you have any issues tracking your order, please contact our customer support team.
